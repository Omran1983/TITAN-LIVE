# DISCIPLINARY & HR LETTER TEMPLATES
**Scope:** Private Sector Mauritius

---

## 1. LETTER OF EXPLANATION (DEMANDE D'EXPLICATION)
**Use when:** investigating a potential minor misconduct.

**Date:** [Date]
**To:** [Employee Name]

**Subject: Request for Explanation**

Dear [Name],

It has been brought to our attention that [describe incident - e.g., you were absent without authorization on Monday 12th].

You are hereby requested to provide a written explanation regarding this matter within **[24/48] hours** of receiving this letter.

Failure to provide a satisfactory explanation may result in further disciplinary action.

Regards,
Management

---

## 2. WRITTEN WARNING (AVERTISSEMENT)
**Use when:** employee has committed a fault or explanation was rejected.

**Date:** [Date]
**To:** [Employee Name]

**Subject: Written Warning**

Dear [Name],

Following your letter of explanation dated [Date] regarding [incident], management finds your explanation unsatisfactory for the following reasons:
1. [Reason 1]
2. [Reason 2]

This letter serves as a **Formal Written Warning**.
We expect an immediate improvement in your [conduct/attendance]. Please be advised that further misconduct of a similar nature may lead to more severe disciplinary actions, up to and including termination.

A copy of this warning will be placed in your personnel file.

Regards,
Management
