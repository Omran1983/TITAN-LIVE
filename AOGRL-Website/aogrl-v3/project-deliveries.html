Overview

Who AOGRL Deliveries is for

Ideal clients

  • Fashion boutiques and clothing shops.
  • Small electronics and accessories retailers.
  • Online sellers using Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp.
  • Health & wellness product resellers.

How the service runs

  • You book a run via WhatsApp, phone, or agreed channel.
  • We plan routes and drivers inside Delivery_CRM.
  • Driver picks up from your stock location or shop.
  • We deliver to your customer and confirm status back to you.
