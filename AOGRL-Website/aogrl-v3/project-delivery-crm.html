Delivery_CRM

Delivery_CRM is the specialised slice of AOGRL CRM designed purely for the delivery side: drivers, routes, runs, fuel and proof of delivery.

  • Daily routes and run sheets.
  • Driver and vehicle assignment.
  • Basic fuel and cost tracking.

What Delivery_CRM is optimised for

Operational detail

Costs & tracking

  • Fuel usage approximations per run.
  • Simple calculations for profitability per route.
  • Exportable data for manual review.
