Jarvis & AION-ZERO

Jarvis is the command center; AION-ZERO is the orchestrator. Together they manage scripts, Workers, deployments, checks and reports for AOGRL projects.

  • Runs PowerShell tasks on a schedule (status, heartbeats, refresh).
  • Talks to Supabase, Cloudflare Workers and local file systems.

Role

What Jarvis & AION-ZERO actually do

Jarvis

  • Executes PowerShell scripts on schedule.
  • Checks Supabase tables and logs status.
  • Deploys or patches Cloudflare Workers.
  • Writes logs, snapshots and simple reports.

AION-ZERO

  • Decides which tasks to run and when.
  • Tracks project states and “commands” in databases.
  • Acts as the meta-layer over Jarvis and other agents.
