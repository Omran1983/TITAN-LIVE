EduConnect

EduConnect is AOGRL’s initiative to connect students, learners, SMEs and training opportunities. It aims to manage outreach, events, enrollments and follow-up in a structured way.

  • Tracks schools, events, attendees and follow-ups.
  • Manages communication with students and SMEs.
  • Powered by Supabase, Jarvis and AOGRL’s automation stack.

Scope

What EduConnect will handle

Core objects

  • Institutions (schools, colleges, training partners).
  • Students and participants.
  • Events, workshops and sessions.
  • Tasks and follow-ups.

Automations

  • Email / WhatsApp reminders for events.
  • Post-event follow-up flows.
  • Reporting dashboards for attendance and outcomes.
Status

Internal system in progress

Infrastructure

  • Supabase-based backend and schema.
  • Cloudflare Workers & automation scripts managed by Jarvis.
  • Front-end being iterated within AOGRL’s UI stack.

Public availability

  • Currently internal to AOGRL.
  • No public URL yet (by design).
  • Potential future offering to schools and agencies.
