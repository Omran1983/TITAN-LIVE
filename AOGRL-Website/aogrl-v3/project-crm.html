Scope

What AOGRL CRM manages

Data model

  • Clients (businesses, shops, partners).
  • Customers (end buyers linked to clients).
  • Orders and line items.
  • Drivers and vehicles (linked to Delivery_CRM).
  • Routes and daily runs.
  • Invoices (email + PDF).

Planned & existing features

  • Email invoicing and basic email campaigns.
  • WhatsApp / SMS notifications via integrated providers.
  • Map-based planning for routes and client clusters.
  • Analytics dashboards for performance and profitability.
