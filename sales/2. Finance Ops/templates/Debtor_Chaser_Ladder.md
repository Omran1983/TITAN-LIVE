# Debtor Chaser Ladder (WhatsApp + Email)
**Goal:** Recover cash without ruining relationships.
**Mechanism:** 3-Step Escalation.
**Automation:** Client sends "Unpaid List" -> Agent sends these messages.

---

## Level 1: The "Did you forget?" (Day +1 after Due Date)
*Tone: Helpful, Polite. Assumes it slipped their mind.*

**WhatsApp:**
> "Hi [Name], hope you're having a good week! Just a gentle nudge on Invoice [Inv#] for [Amount] which was due yesterday. Let me know if you need the bank details again? Thanks, [Your Name]"

**Email Subject: Quick reminder: Invoice [Inv#]**
> Hi [Name],
>
> Hope you're well.
>
> Just bringing Invoice [Inv#] (Due: [Date]) to the top of your inbox. It keeps our cashflow tidy if you could settle it today.
>
> Bank: [Bank Name] | Acc: [Account Number]
>
> Thanks!
> [Your Name]

---

## Level 2: The "Is there an issue?" (Day +7 Overdue)
*Tone: Professional, Direct. Asking for a timeline.*

**WhatsApp:**
> "Hi [Name], we haven't received payment for Invoice [Inv#] yet. Is there an issue with the invoice or bank transfer? Please let me know today so I can update accounts. Thanks."

**Email Subject: Overdue: Invoice [Inv#] - Action Required**
> Hi [Name],
>
> We note that Invoice [Inv#] for [Amount] is now 7 days overdue.
>
> Can you please confirm if this has been scheduled for payment? If you need a copy of the invoice re-sent, just let me know.
>
> Please settle to: [Account Number]
>
> Regards,
> [Your Name]

---

## Level 3: The "Final Notice" (Day +14 Overdue)
*Tone: Serious. Implies stopping service.*

**WhatsApp:**
> "Hi [Name], Invoice [Inv#] is now significantly overdue. To avoid any service interruption, please settle the [Amount] immediately and send proof of payment. Thank you."

**Email Subject: URGENT: Outstanding Balance on Account [Acc#]**
> Hi [Name],
>
> This is a formal notice that Invoice [Inv#] remains unpaid despite previous reminders.
>
> **Amount Due:** [Amount]
> **Status:** 14 Days Overdue
>
> Please arrange immediate payment to prevent this account being flagged for credit hold.
>
> Regards,
> Accounts Team
