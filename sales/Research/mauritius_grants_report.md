### 1. Technology and Innovation Scheme (TINNS) - **HIGH PRIORITY**
**What it is:** A grant covering **80% of costs** (up to Rs 150,000) for digital solutions.
**Why it matters:**
*   **For AOGRL:** You can apply to fund internal tech (CRM, Automation tools).
*   **For Clients:** You can "sell" your ComplianceOps service as part of a digital transformation package if you bundle it with software, or advise them to use this grant to pay for the initial setup/digitization.
*   **Covered:** Websites, ERP, Accounting Software, **Process Automation**.

### 2. Innovation Boost Grant (MRIC)
**What it is:** Matching grant up to **Rs 1,000,000** for projected 12-month innovation projects.
**Target:** Innovative products/services. "ComplianceOps" (AI-driven compliance) could qualify as an "e-innovation".
**Deadline:** March 28, 2025.
**Action:** Consider submitting a proposal for the "AI Compliance Agents" as a funded R&D project.

### 3. SME Marketing & Visibility Scheme
**What it is:** Grants to support marketing materials, trade fairs, and digital visibility.
**Relevance:** Use this to subsidize the cost of your "Social Media Flyers" or running a booth at the *Salon Des PME* (next one in Flacq).
