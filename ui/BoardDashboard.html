Strategy & Systems Director

Automation Backbone Jarvis · AION-ZERO
Objectives & Strategy
Objective: Turn AOGRL into a self-running automation ecosystem where agents and scripts handle recurring work.

Strategy: Standardise all projects (EduConnect, OKASINA, ReachX, AION-ZERO) into reusable modules: scheduled tasks, workers, PS scripts, Supabase tables, health checks and daily board reports.

2) Add a flag for failures and missed runs.
3) Render a traffic-light view (green/amber/red) in a small dashboard.
